Eco-Vigilante

The tactic

In this mandatory activity, students have to form into groups of 4-5 and take turns participating in energy-saving activities at school. Their actions will be tracked using a checklist and self-reflection document on Google Docs. Students will record their energy-saving efforts, such as turning off lights or ceiling fans in classrooms when not in use.
