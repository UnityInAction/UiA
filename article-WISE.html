Energy Wise Poster Campaign

P5 Activities 2024

Another competition students can participate in is a poster-making contest with the theme "Satu Tindakan, Banyak Manfaat." Two groups of students from each class, each consisting of four members, will create a digital A3 poster that includes a call to action, relevant images depicting energy conservation actions, and additional facts or statistics about the energy savings associated with those actions.
