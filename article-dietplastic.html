Diet Plastic

P5 activities 2024.

From March through April, each student will receive a stamp card with 10 slots, and by making eco-friendly choices, they can earn a stamp each week. To collect a stamp, students must visit the designated booth at the school canteen, staffed by two committee members, and show that they are using alternatives to plastic. Acceptable alternatives include bringing a reusable tumbler or lunchbox instead of single-use plastic or using their own tote bag or cutlery. To pass the challenge, students must collect at least 6 stamps. The first 10 students from each grade who fill their cards with all 10 stamps by Earth Day will receive a special prize.
