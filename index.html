Who We Are

What is Unity in Action?

Unity in Action (UiA), founded in 2023, is a student-led organization committed to promoting environmental sustainability and social welfare in Tangerang Selatan.

Through various initiatives, including awareness campaigns, community service projects, and collaborations with local organizations, UiA empowers individuals to take meaningful action. With a focus on education, advocacy, and hands-on involvement, UiA strives to create a lasting impact and inspire a sense of responsibility toward a better, more sustainable future.

Meet Our Symbol

Our Mascot

Unity in Action's mascot, known as the Unitree, is an apple tree with a bright smile plastered on its stem and sturdy branches acting as its arms. This little creature represents the environment as a whole, happy to be fostered and taken care of as our community's main goal. On its head are three apples, which represent unity and the perfect balance between caring for the environment, thinking about ways to help, and finally, doing things to help the environment.




Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Nurturing a safe and habitable environment by creating a habit of sustainable living across individuals and communities.

Our Mission

To achieve our vision, Unity in Action is in pursuit of accomplishing various activities, ranging from afforestation to attending events, that will help in promoting the habit and knowledge of living a sustainable lifestyle across individuals and communities.
